Wilde Cafe ☕

Supply Chain Challenges Simulation

How to play

You manage coffee bean orders between Wilde Cafe and their supplier. Real-world disruptions will hit at unexpected points — minimise inventory while staying profitable.

1
Order — decide how many kg to order (delivered next week, max 30 kg).
2
Supplier processes — 1-week lead time, up to 30 kg/week.
3
Receive — delivery added to stock at the start of the following week.
4
Sell — shortages mean lost sales while overheads still apply.
After the sim, match each challenge to a category: Competitors Volatile environment Robustness Supply Chain Fads Supply disruptions Lack of good people Sustainability
