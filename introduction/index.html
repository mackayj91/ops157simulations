Welcome! You manage coffee orders between a small coffee shop, Wilde Cafe and their supplier of coffee beans! Across the session, you will practice managing this business as it gets more and more complicated with elements of supply chain management. The goal of this first simulation is to manage how much to order, balancing inventory, costs and profit/loss. Where should you start from? Entirely up to you! Hint: you most likely will go out of business. If you are unsure where to begin, just press 'next week'.
